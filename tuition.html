Tuition

Tuition Cost of Course

  • Application Fee (non-refundable)…………………………………….. $50.00
  • Tuition………………………………………………………………… $2,700.00
  • Books and extra equipment (approximately)……………………….. $600.00
  • Downpayment (cash or money order)………………………………. $650.00

    • Payment Schedule

    Per the school contract, tuition is expected the first of the month, however these benchmarks will determine a students continuation when a regular payment schedule is not kept.

  • 750 hours…………………..Balance…………… $1,350.00
  • 1000 hours…………………Balance…………… $1,125.00
  • 1400 hours…………………Balance……………. $ 225.00

    • If financial obligations are not kept, student will be interrupted, after 15 days of interruption student will be charged a reinstatement fee of $100.00 Payments may be made with personal checks to the school, however if the check does not clear student will be assessed a fee of 35.00 along with recovery of the payment in cash or a money order. The down payment and last payment, due no later than 1400 hours, must be paid with cash or a money order. Prepayment Schedule Students enrolled for at least 5% (75hrs) of the program, the tuition by the school shall be as follows when there is a prepayment. Over

  • 5.0% (75 hours) to 9.9% (149 hours) 30% of Tuition*
  • 10.0% (150 hours) to 14.9% (225 hours) 40% of Tuition*
  • 15.0% (225 hours) to 24.9% (374 hours) 45% of Tuition*
  • 25.0% (450 hours) to 49.9% (749 hours) 70% of Tuition*
  • 50.0% (750 hours) and over 100% of Tuition*
